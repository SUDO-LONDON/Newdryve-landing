Appendix · Q&A
newdryve.

The hard questions.

Why won't TotalDrive just copy this?
Their DNA is admin software. The hard part isn't a calendar, it's building local supply/demand density, trust, verification and cancellation enforcement. We start with the marketplace problem on day one.
Why won't AA or RED crush you?
They're franchises tied to their own branded ecosystems. Newdryve is for the independent majority who don't want to give up pricing, payments and their student relationships.
Why will instructors pay £29/month?
TotalDrive already proves they pay. We add learner acquisition and cancellation protection, one booking or one protected cancellation can pay for itself.
Why no commission?
Commission misaligns us with the instructor. We want to be trusted infrastructure, not a tax on every lesson, subscription gives us predictable SaaS revenue instead.
How do you prevent disintermediation?
The booking is only the entry point. Diary, progress, protection, earnings, receipts and exports become the system of record, leaving gets painful.
Is this venture scale?
The UK car-ADI market is the beachhead. The opportunity is the operating layer for independent instructors, expanding into European markets, plus insurance and vehicle products.
